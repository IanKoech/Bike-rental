Our Mission

We are an organisation that provides bike rental services. You can get in touch with a lessor depending on the location you are at at the moment. The lessors charge usage hourly, though since these are trying times, charges may be discounted depending on the location and time of usage.

Our Approach

Connecting

Patnership is key to everything we do.We recognise that in order to provide people with one of the best tramsport services on the go, collaboration is key. We work with many and verifed individuals and organisations to break down the artificial barriers that restrict easy and seamless transportation, so that people of all ages have the chance to be as healthy and enjoy bike rides in groups or individually at very affordable.

Supporting

We believe in the potential of people to make healthy positive changes. We at Ollies aim to support people who are looking to get an extra source of income from owning bicycles and enabling them to lease them to our verified and trusted customers when they are not in use.

Specialties

We specialize in higher end bike rentals to cruise and also provide private rides to the Karura Fores. We provide free bicycle delivery and pickup anywhere within Nairobi. We pride ourselves in providing excellent customer service- we bring the bikes to you and pick them up at your convenience, or you can drop them off at various locations. We pick you up at your hotel/home for our tours and drop you back off at the same location. Our tours are not more than 3-5 persons at a time, so they are more like an adventure than a trip. Give us a call and see how we can make your riding experience better.
